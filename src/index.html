About the Company

A Legacy of Clean Energy in the Himalayas

Bhujung Hydropower Limited is a Nepal-based hydropower developer with a singular mission: to harness the abundant water resources of the Gandaki river system to deliver clean, reliable electricity for generations to come.

Registered in Kathmandu and operating in Lamjung District, we combine deep local expertise with rigorous engineering standards to develop projects that benefit communities, investors, and the environment alike.

Project Overview &
Salient Features

A meticulously engineered run-of-river hydroelectric plant delivering clean power to Nepal's national grid.

Mathillo Midim Hydroelectric Project

arrow_forward Bhujung, Lamjung District · Gandaki Province
Under Construction

A 7.5 MW run-of-river hydroelectric project engineered to generate 42.167 GWh of clean energy annually. Strategically sited on the Midim Khola in Lamjung, this project leverages a gross head of 202 metres and a design discharge of 4.75 m³/s. With minimal ecological impact, it aligns with Nepal's national energy security vision and NEA grid integration mandate.

Zero Carbon Operations

Run-of-river design produces no greenhouse gas emissions during generation. Every kilowatt displaces fossil fuel alternatives, actively reducing Nepal's carbon footprint and contributing to global climate goals.

Eco-Friendly Design

No reservoir impoundment — the run-of-river system diverts only the necessary minimum water flow, preserving downstream ecosystems, aquatic biodiversity, and the natural morphology of Midim Khola.

100%
Environmental compliance certified

Community Development

Active investment in the Bhujung community: local employment, infrastructure support, schools, roads, and healthcare facilities. Reforestation programs plant over 2,500 trees annually in Lamjung district.

Regulatory Compliance

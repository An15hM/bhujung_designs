About the Company

A Legacy of Clean Energy in the Himalayas

Bhujung Hydropower Limited is a Nepal-based hydropower developer with a singular mission: to harness the abundant water resources of the Gandaki river system to deliver clean, reliable electricity for generations to come.

Registered in Kathmandu and operating in Lamjung District, we combine deep local expertise with rigorous engineering standards to develop projects that benefit communities, investors, and the environment alike.

  • arrow_forward
    Registered under Companies Act of Nepal, headquartered in Tokha-10, Kathmandu
  • arrow_forward
    Fully licensed by the Department of Electricity Development, Government of Nepal
  • arrow_forward
    Run-of-river design with zero reservoir impoundment — minimal ecological footprint
  • arrow_forward
    Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) secured with Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA)
  • arrow_forward
    Committed to community employment, local procurement, and social investment
